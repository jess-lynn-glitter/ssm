Video Visits

Scheduled or on-demand video visits are now available from home, work, or while on-the-go. Video visits, also known as telehealth or virtual visits, allow you to meet with Piedmont providers from the comfort of your home via smart phone, tablet, or computer. Remember that a valid ID, insurance card and form of payment is required at time of service.

All locations are cashless as of January 2023

We accept most major credit cards, debit cards, FSA/HSA cards, gift cards (Visa and Mastercard) and personal checks that represent the exact amount for services. Remember that a valid ID, insurance card and form of payment is required at time of services. Without these necessary documents, your visit will be rescheduled.
